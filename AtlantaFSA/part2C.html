About Culture Shock

Culture Shock was established in 2002 by Georgia State and Georgia Tech FSA as a way to bring the community out for a night of food, culture, and entertainment. 2016 marks fourteen years since Culture Shock's conception and the tradition is going strong!

This year's theme is Fuse. What do we mean by "fuse"? Stay tuned!
