About Culture Shock

Culture Shock was established in 2002 by Georgia State and Georgia Tech FSA as a way to bring the community out for a night of food, culture, and entertainment. 2016 marks fourteen years since Culture Shock's conception and the tradition is going strong!

Culture Shock: Fuse gives performers an opportunity to showcase multiple skills or different genres in the talent you choose. Incorporating an additional skill or genre represents how different cultures today are able to come together and work as one.
