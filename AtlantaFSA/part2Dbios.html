GSU Delta Phi Lambda Sorority Inc (Exhibition)

Delta Phi Lambda Sorority, Inc. was founded at the University of Georgia on December 5, 1998 and chartered at Georgia State University on February 4, 2001. Along with 16 other undergraduate chapters across the nation, Delta Phi Lambda advocates Asian awareness and empowers women to lead through values-based programs and everlasting sisterhood. Also known as Dedicated Female Leaders, these ladies will be performing the art of strolling: a form of dancing in a line.

Kate Du Aime Russell (Competition)

Kate was born in Vietnam, adopted and raised by her Franco-American parents, has lived in seven different countries, and grew up surrounded by different cultures. She says, since she’s lived such an international life, she has struggled to identify with one specific culture. However three dominant cultures currently in her life are: German, French and American.

Kavi Vu (Competition)

Kavi Vu is a spoken word artist from Atlanta. She considers herself a storyteller who aims to inspire her audience with her passion for creative freedom, social justice, and culture. With her journalism background from the University of Georgia, she aspires to tell the stories of those who cannot tell it themselves. She believes that too many ideas are left unspoken and too many stories are going untold from generation to generation. She hopes to bridge that gap.

Impact (Exhibition)

Impact is a dance team part of the Christian ministry, KCCC, which consists of multiple college campuses across Atlanta. Impact's purpose is to not dance to impress, but to dance to express and share a meaningful impactful message. The performance theme of this semester uses dance to share a powerful message to inspire, challenge, and encourage people to make an impact and be a light in their communities.

TNT (Competition)

Created in Fall of 2009, TrickaNomeTry (TNT) Dance Crew is an all-male hip hop dance crew coming out of Emory University. TNT originally started as a group of friends: some were dancers, the others trickers. Over the years, TNT has developed an energetic style of dancing, focusing on a diverse range of choreographic styles. Onstage, TNT strives both to entertain audiences and to have fun doing it.
