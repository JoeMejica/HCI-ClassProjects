About

The new Georgia History Museum (GHM) located in Atlanta, GA. The museum will showcase the history of Georgia from the founding of Savannah in 1733 to present day. The GHM will also cover the culture of the Native American tribes that existed in what is now Georgia. The GHM will have both coventional exhibits, e.g., documents, drawings, and photographs as well as scale models of some of the important Forts, e.g., Fort Frederica. The GHM is expected to open Fall 2018 and the proposed website is expected to launch late Spring 2017.

Goals

The primary goal of the Georgia Hisotry Muesum is to serve as a local epicenter of historical education for the Atlanta and metro-Atlanta area. The museum hopes to become an educational landmark for all education levels as well as a vacation destination for those visiting Atlanta. With the existence of attractions such as the Georgia Aquarium, World of Coke, Centennial Olympic Park, the muesum will add an additional layer of excitement to the ever growing community in Atlanta.

Website Objective

The Georgia History Musuem's website will be the #1 information resource regarding the museum's attractions, amentities, events, contacts, and more. With the advancement of web technologies, the developers behind the website will not only be current with today's demands in web information, but serve as a platform to not only learn about the museum but invoke excitement for future visitors to come.

Services

While the website will serve as an information hub about the museum, the website plans to offer a variety of services on its website such as an online store for musuem merchandise, special event registration, career portal for upcoming job opportunities, and more.

Targeted Demographics

Users Focus

The primary user focus is learning about the Georgia History Museum, their services and events, and the history of Georgia.

User Goals and Objectives

After navigating the user and mobile-friendly website, users should become more knowledgeable about the Muesum and be able to translate this information to friends and family and become more attracted to visit the museum.

User Tasks

The user simply has to navigate to the URL and navigate the easy to read website, and obtain the user's desired information.
