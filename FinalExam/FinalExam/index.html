Request for Proposal

Welcome

This is a Request for Proposal for the new Georgia History Museum (GHM) located in Atlanta, GA. The museum will showcase the history of Georgia from the founding of Savannah in 1733 to present day. The GHM will also cover the culture of the Native American tribes that existed in what is now Georgia. The GHM will have both coventional exhibits, e.g., documents, drawings, and photographs as well as scale models of some of the important Forts, e.g., Fort Frederica. The GHM is expected to open Fall 2018 and the proposed website is expected to launch late Spring 2017.

This website is to propose, inform, and plan the overall design and objective of the museum's website. JM Development has been contracted to be the primary web developer/architect in addition of utilizing the company's information technology services thorughout the faciltiy. Entailed in the website is demographic information of the website's target audience, additioanl information about GHM, the high level design and prototype of the website, as well as a dedicated page on the utilization of VR and AR technology in the museum to enhance the user experience.
